StartOS is an open-source Linux distribution that turns commodity hardware into a fully managed personal server. It handles service discovery, installation, networking, backups, and health monitoring — so you stay in control of your data without needing to be a systems administrator.

## What you can do with StartOS

StartOS lets you replace cloud services with self-hosted alternatives you fully own. Browse and install services from the [Start9 Marketplace](https://marketplace.start9.com/) — including Bitcoin nodes, Lightning wallets, encrypted messaging, and cloud storage — all from a single web interface.
